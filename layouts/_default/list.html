About

The Soil Factory is a vibrant community space where art, science and sustainability come together to create new collaborations, new experiences and new ideas. We celebrate experimental learning while exploring new partnerships. We are artists, students, scientists, farmers, entrepreneurs, and interested members of the community who meet to discuss, play, learn, and seek new solutions for the issues of our day. We are a place where everyone is welcome.

Founded in Ithaca, NY, in mid-2021 to serve as an experimental space to develop innovative ways of bringing a diverse group of people together to tackle the most pressing issues of our time. The Soil Factory explores art-science approaches together with the local community, policy makers, the academic community, and anyone in the Ithaca region and globally who are interested in building an unusual platform of creative engagement.

We are organized by WeaveCommunity, an Ithaca-based community organization. This website lists programming, links to information of those engaging with this initiative, and invites your participation.
